1. Deploy Targets

Click "Deploy Target" to place your marker on the map. Each target becomes a battle target for other players.

2. Select Target & Range

Click a target on the map to see its age, distance, and value. Choose a range (500km, 1000km, 4000km, or Global) to filter which targets are valid.

3. Launch Attacks

Pick an attack type, then hit ATTACK. Each attack has a cooldown and a cost, so timing matters.

4. Points & Score

Destroying targets earns points based on target age and defenses. Spend points to launch stronger attacks or upgrade defenses.

5. Build Defenses

Upgrade Shield (extra HP), Armor (damage reduction), and Interceptors (block chance) to protect your targets.

6. Interceptors & Shield Recharge

Interceptors can auto-block incoming attacks and recharge over time. Shields recharge after a short delay once damage stops.

7. Battle Stats

Track Session time, Kills, Avg DMG, Accuracy, Attacks sent, Score, and Points spent. Hover each stat for details.

8. Battle Feed

The feed shows recent attacks, kills, and key events with attack type and damage.

9. Auto-Agents & Cooldowns

Auto-agents also attack on the map. After you launch an attack, a short cooldown runs before you can attack again.
